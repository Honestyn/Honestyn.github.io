3-D Printing Will Change the World

The word “3-D printing” can appear intimidating and futuristic at first glance, but it is a simple advancement in the current toner and ink printer world. It is the enabling of a machine to produce three-dimensional objects in the comfort of one’s home or office. 3-d printing offers an array of benefits. It will allow consumers to create and build their own objects which could decrease production costs, by eliminating shipping, handling, etc. It also allows builders to express their creativity efficiently by changing the design or function of an object by changing software instructions, vs retooling. The new era of 3-d printing will change the way companies, consumers, and inventors conduct business. Internet of Things

The Internet of Things deals with the increase of machine-to-machine communication. It uses cloud computing and networks of data-gathering sensors. Internet of Things seeks to making all objects especially everyday objects “smarter”. To make machines smarter, sensors are used to collect data. The data is then leveraged using cloud based applications, which transmits and interprets the data. Experts argue that the Internet of Things is the biggest current tech trend, because it will open the doors for the most opportunities in the near future.
